Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is this site?

    2. This website is made for Tracking and organizing College laboratories.

  2. How to report a broken PC?

    3. From the navigation bar you can click on report button then you will be directed to the report page.

  3. How can I find the broken computers in my lab?

    4. From the navigation bar you can click on broken computers button then you will be directed to the broken computers page where you can find all the broken computers in the entire labs.

  4. How to search for a computer or a lab?

    5. From the navigation bar you will click on search button then you will be directed to the search page where you can filter labs and computers.

  5. How can I edit my lab info?

    6. Only the admins can edit the labs so you can contact your lab admin to edit something in the lab.
